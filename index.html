Welcome to
Rescue Me Georgia

RESCUE ME GEORGIA is a 501c3 ALL VOLUNTEER non-profit rescue. RMG is foster based and oh, what a team we have ! NO ONE is paid - so every donations goes DIRECTLY to take care of the dogs we save. PLEASE check us out at facebook.com/rescuemega to see what’s is happening daily! We can’t do this without your support - thank you for sharing and caring and turning on friends to our rescue group. Our ability to save more innocent lives hinges upon donations and volunteers. To learn more about what our rescue is doing on a daily basis, please like our Facebook page by clicking HERE. Please also review our web site, which is loaded with information, applications to foster or adopt, and a donation link. We realize we can’t change a dog’s past, but with your support, we can certainly re-write his or her future!

App is wondering where everyone is?!?! Our dogs can provide immeasurable comfort as we make our way through these trying times so at RMG we are doing what we can to maintain some basic operations while balancing that with the safety and health of our volunteers and potential adopters. With that in mind, we are not allowing any out of state adoptions. Adoptions are restricted to the Metro Atlanta Area only.

WANT TO HELP?

Please consider making a tax-deductible donation to support our mission using our DONATE link. We are a licensed 501 c(3) nonprofit organization run entirely by volunteers, so all funds raised are applied directly to dogs in need. (All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent of the law.) Please review the links on our web site to see how you can become part of the RMG family.
